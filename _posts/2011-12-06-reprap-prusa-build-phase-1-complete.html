RepRap Prusa Build Phase 1

The instructions I followed were the Prusa Mendel Assembly instructions, with modifications described in the Thames Valley Google Groups thread Frame bearing specifics?.

This Prusa is 16mm wider than standard, in order to allow for the heated bed. For further details on the design being followed, please visit http://tvrrug.org.uk/.
